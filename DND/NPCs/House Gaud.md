# House Gaud

The Baron and Baroness Gaud of the [[Barony of Marrant]], [[Kingdom of Kessenval]] live in [[Oak-s Memory Manor]], near the town of [[Loran-s Oaks]] and the [[Marolin Forest]].

## Baroness Inessa Gaud

Human. Early fourties. Brown hair tied back in a loose bun.  
Benefactor of archeological endeavors and collector of rare ancient items.
Wife of Lukam.

## Baron Lukam Gaud

Baron of Husband of Inessa.
