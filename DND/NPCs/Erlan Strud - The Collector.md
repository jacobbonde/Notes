# Erlan Strud - The Collector

Human. Roughly 50. Short and a bit stout. Balding, dark hair.  

Collector of rare items. Wants to buy the [[Idol of Gundran]] from [[House Gaud#Baroness Inessa Gaud]].

Erlan's fascination with the idol awakens a spark of [[God-King Gundran]]'s god-spirit.
