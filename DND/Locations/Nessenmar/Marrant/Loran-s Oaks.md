# Loran's Oaks

Town in [[Barony of Marrant]], [[Kingdom of Nessenmar]] near [[Oak-s Memory Manor]] and about a days travel from [[King Alkan-s tomb]].

The town is built around a town square with a huge solitary oak tree (Loran's Great Oak) in the center.

## Points of Interest

### The Great Oak Market

Local market in the middle of the town, around the Great Oak.

### Orster Smithy

The sisters Nolina (blacksmith and farrier) and Gimma (weaponsmith and armourer) run the Orster Smithy. Apprenticies (strikers).
