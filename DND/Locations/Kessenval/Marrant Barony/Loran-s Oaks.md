## Description
The town is built around a town square with a huge solitary oak tree (Loran's Great Oak) in the centre.

## Points of Interest

### The Great Oak Market
*Markedet ved den store Eg*
Local market in the middle of the town, around the Great Oak. In the oak lives large, shiny blue beetles.

### The Temple
*Templet*
A large round building. The local priest is a rotund, balding man.

### Orster Smithy
*Orster-smedjen*
The sisters **Nolina** (dark hair tied back ) (blacksmith and farrier) and **Gimma** (curly red hair) (weaponsmith and armourer) run the Orster Smithy. Apprenticies (strikers).

### The Acorn Inn 
*Agernkroen*
The Inn is run by the **Limeram** family. 
Turnspit dogs? or just a kid?

### Vona's Grove
*Vona i Hegnet*
The wise woman [[Vona in the Hedge]] lives in a hut in a grove close to the town.
