# Oak's Memory Manor

The manor of [[House Gaud]], situated near the town of [[Loran-s Oaks]], in the [[Barony of Marrant]], in [[Kingdom of Kessenval]]

The Manor also houses the Gaud Collection of Rare Artifacts.
