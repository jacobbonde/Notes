# Loran's Oaks

Town in [[Barony of Marrant]], [[Kingdom of Kessenval]] near [[Oak-s Memory Manor]] and about a days travel from [[King Alkan-s tomb]].

The town is built around a town square with a huge solitary oak tree (Loran's Great Oak) in the center.

## Points of Interest

### The Great Oak Market
Markedet ved den store Eg
Local market in the middle of the town, around the Great Oak.

### Orster Smithy
Orster-smedjen
The sisters Nolina (dark hair tied back ) (blacksmith and farrier) and Gimma (curly red hair) (weaponsmith and armourer) run the Orster Smithy. Apprenticies (strikers).

### The Acorn Inn
